# Campus Contributor Documentation

These pages are intended for current and potential campus contributors to the
Open Science Data Federation (OSDF) and
Open Science Pool (OSPool),
including cluster administrators,
project PIs and leadership, and
campus staff who facilitate the use of clusters.

Please [let us know](mailto:support@osg-htc.org)
how we can improve these pages to better support you!


## Open Science Data Federation (OSDF)

The [Open Science Data Federation (OSDF)](https://osg-htc.org/services/osdf)
connects disparate dataset repositories into
a single, nationwide data distribution network.
Campuses can provide storage to back data distribution services,
serve their own datasets, 
or share other public data objects.

**Do you have storage to integrate with the OSDF?**

Get started on our [OSDF integration page](osdf-integration.md).

> Our OSDF integration process is still in development.
> If you cannot find the information you need on this website or other web presences,
> please [email us](mailto:support@osg-htc.org).

## Open Science Pool (OSPool)

The [Open Science Pool](https://osg-htc.org/services/ospool/) (OSPool)
presents a unified means of accessing
computing capacity that is shared from campuses nationwide
via a single HTCondor pool.
This pool is accessible to any researcher affiliated with a US academic institution.

**Want to contribute to the OSPool from your campus?**

Get started on our [OSPool integration page](ospool-integration.md).

**Already contributing to the OSPool?**

*   [Learn more](ospool-metrics.md) about your contributions and how researchers benefit from them
*   [See how](ospool-metrics.md#investigating-live-ospool-jobs) to investigate live OSPool jobs on your system
*   [Check](ospool-maintenance.md) our list of suggested maintenance tasks and when to contact us about the integration


## Community

**Participate in the community of OSDF and OSPool campus contributors!**

There are several ways to participate:

*   Join our **Campus Contributors mailing list** (a Google Group).
    We send one or two emails per week, mostly just announcements of the Campus Meet-Ups.
    [Email us](mailto:support@osg-htc.org) with email addresses to add.

*   Participate in the **weekly Campus Meet-Ups** on Zoom,
    Wednesdays at 11:00am to noon, US Central Time.
    The virtual meeting link is included in email announcements,
    or [email us](mailto:support@osg-htc.org) for the link.

*   Join our OSG Consortium **Slack Workspace**;
    [email us](mailto:support@osg-htc.org) with email addresses to invite.

*   Attend our annual Throughput Computing Week event.
    [HTC26](https://osg-htc.org/events/2026/06/09/throughput-computing-week/)
    will be held 9–12 June 2026 at the University of Wisconsin–Madison.

*   If suitable, apply to join
    [the OSG School](https://osg-htc.org/community/school.html).
